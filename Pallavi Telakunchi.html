About Me

Welcome to my world of creativity! I'm Pallavi Telakunchi, a passionate individual with a diverse set of skills and a relentless drive to innovate.

My journey began in the world of graphic design, where I honed my skills in visual storytelling and brand communication. Over time, my fascination with technology led me to explore the realm of front-end development, where I found my true calling.

With a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of user experience principles, I strive to craft digital experiences that not only look stunning but also engage and delight users.

When I'm not immersed in pixels and code, you'll often find me lost in the pages of a good book, exploring nature's beauty through photography, or indulging my curiosity with DIY projects.
